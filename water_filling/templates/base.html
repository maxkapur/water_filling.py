Water filling

This site demonstrates the water-filling problem: Given an uneven tub defined by a list of heights and a certain volume of water, what will the level of the water be once it has sloshed around and stabilized?

The water-filling problem can be solved by deriving the function for volume(heights, level), the volume of water present when the level is at a given value. Since this function is monotonically increasing in level, we can find the level that contains the target volume from the original problem using a root-finding algorithm like bisection or the secant method used here.
